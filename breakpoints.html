What is breakpoints and why do we use them?

Everyday websites are accessed by people all over the world via different devices that have various screen sizes and resolutions. To secure that text and images are not obscured in any way when opening a website with different formats, the creation of CSS breakpoints has been a revelation. By adding these points within the code, they can communicate with the website and then its content adjusts itself to fit the display that is being used. This allows a further pleasing experience for users on all platforms.
